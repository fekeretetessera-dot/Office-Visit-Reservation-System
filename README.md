## <u><strong>Overview</strong></u>
The Office Visit Reservation System is a Power Apps + SharePoint solution designed to automate visit scheduling, manage limited office capacity, and streamline all-day work requests.  
This solution was built during COVID-19 to reduce manual coordination workload, prevent double-booking, and ensure compliance with capacity restrictions.

---

## <u><strong>My Role</strong></u>
- Designed the SharePoint lists and data structure for reservations, availability, and equipment tracking  
- Built the Power Apps interface for employees to submit visit requests and all-day work requests  
- Created automated workflows in Power Automate for approvals, reminders, and notifications  
- Implemented timeslot logic to avoid double-booking and manage limited daily capacity  
- Developed automated confirmations, reminders, and cancellation logic  
- Collaborated with supervisors and department leads for requirement gathering, testing, and deployment  

---

## <u><strong>Tools & Technologies</strong></u>
- **Power Apps** (Canvas App for reservation forms and dashboards)  
- **Power Automate** (Approval workflows, notifications, reminders)  
- **SharePoint Online** (Lists, permissions, and data structure)  
- **Microsoft 365** (Teams, Outlook integration for notifications)  

---

## <u><strong>Key Features</strong></u>
- Automated office visit scheduling with defined timeslots  
- All-day work request workflows with multi-stage approvals  
- Daily capacity tracking and management to prevent overbooking  
- Automated confirmations, reminders, and cancellation notifications  
- Dashboard view of scheduled visits and pending approvals  

---

## <u><strong>Key Metrics & Impact</strong></u>
- Reduced manual coordination workload by **70%**  
- Enabled **150+ reservations per month** with zero double-booking  
- Decreased no-shows by **25%** through automated reminders  
- Improved visibility for supervisors on pending requests and office capacity  
- Streamlined the approval process and reduced delays in work scheduling
